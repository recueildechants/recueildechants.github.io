Third-Party Services

How we keep RC free & stable

RC uses two industry-standard services — one to display ads that keep the app free, and one to automatically detect crashes so we can fix them fast. Neither service is operated by RC.

Advertising

Google AdMob

Displays ads so the app remains free for everyone. AdMob may collect:

  • Device advertising identifier (IDFA / GAID)
  • IP address & approximate location
  • Device type & OS version
  • Ad impression & interaction data

Governed by Google's Privacy Policy. Manage your preferences at adssettings.google.com.

Crash Reporting

Firebase Crashlytics

Automatically reports crashes so we can diagnose and fix stability issues quickly. If a crash occurs, Crashlytics collects:

  • Crash stack trace & signal type (e.g. SIGSEGV)
  • App version & build number
  • iOS version & device model
  • Device locale & time zone
  • Timestamp of the crash

No names, emails, or personal identity are ever included. Governed by Google Firebase Privacy Policy.

Privacy

Your privacy matters

RC itself collects no personal data. The only information that ever leaves your device is anonymous crash diagnostics — to help us fix bugs — and advertising signals, both handled entirely by Google's infrastructure.

RC collects: Nothing. No accounts, no personal information, no usage analytics.

Firebase Crashlytics may collect: Anonymous technical data — crash stack traces, device model, OS & app version, locale, time zone, and crash timestamp.

Google AdMob may collect: Device advertising identifiers and interaction data to serve and measure ads.

For full details, please read our Privacy Policy.
